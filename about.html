About MapBiome

A look into the microscopic world

What does MapBiome do?

MapBiome provides digestible information on bacterial profiles for everyday objects. Although countless journal articles and research papers contain information on the microbiomic profiles of numerous surfaces, much of the information is either too difficult to understand or lacks an intriguing factor for the layman. Additionally, self-research into bacterial profiles on search engines leads to articles that highlight the bacterial content with a brief paragraph that does not provide any visual nor comparative data. Thus, mapbiome not only presents data that can be interpreted by the average person, but also gives information on a plethora objects for insightful comparison. There are no websites that provide information solely of various microbiomic profiles for everyday objects.

A note from the creator

Graduating with a Bachelor degree in Molecular and Cell Biology from UC Berkeley at nearly the same time as the Coronavirus Outbreak, it was imperative to create a free website for those who want to learn more about microbial life in our everyday lives. As a researcher and a biology tutor, I set a goal to create digestible information for those who are beginning to delve into the subject of biology. Mapbiome also allows visitors to create their own analysis based on the data that is presented. Thank you for visiting MapBiome. - Ku
