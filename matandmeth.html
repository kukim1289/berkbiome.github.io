Materials and Methods

From swab to data

Simple yet Effective

Zymo Research Corp’s collection swab with Shield was used. Shield was useful in preserving the bacterial swab for up to 30 days for transport. Swabbing was done by moving the swab on the surface for 45 seconds. Genetic information is extracted with the Zymobiomics Miniprep kit. The protocol for DNA extraction can be found on the Zymo Research website. Afterward, 16s sequencing is performed to create a profile of the microbial species in the sample. The overall methodology for MapBiome’s profiles are simple, reliable, and effective in producing data that is easily interpreted.
